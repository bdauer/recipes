Bacon Wrapped Turkey Legs

Ingredients:

12 strips of bacon
4 turkey legs
salt
pepper
other seasonings (to taste, optional)

1. Preheat your oven to 300 degrees.

2. Salt, pepper and season turkey legs.

3. Place turkey legs on the pan in which they will be cooked.

4. Wrap each turkey leg in 3 strips of bacon, beginning at one end and working your way
to the other end.

5. Put the pan with the wrapped turkey legs in the oven.

6. Bake for 2 hours.

7. Raise heat to 350 degrees.

8. Bake for another 20 minutes. This is how your bacon gets crispy.

9. Remove from oven. Allow to cool a little bit. Enjoy!
